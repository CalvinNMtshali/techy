This Internet Router has beamforming technology and four antennas that give you a premium internet experience. The Antenna design makes your internet connection more efficient and stable.  This Innovative Antenna Design allows you to reach a larger WiFi cover in your home or office.

Modem Compatibility


Please ensure you have verified your connection setup with your ISP before installing this router. Some setups will use combination routers that have both modem and router functions. The Ultra-link WF1200 router does NOT include a built-in modem function. To connect the Ultra-link WF1200 router and utilise your Internet from your ISP you will need a standalone modem device to convert the data from your ISP, most ISP’s will supply this device upon installation. The Ultra-link WF1200 must be connected via LAN cable to the standalone modem device and not directly to your ISP line. To support ADSL, you will need an ADSL modem. To support Fibre, you will need a Fibre modem. To support 4G you will need a 4G modem or 4G mobile router as all these networks use different modems.
